# Bear Warning

**Bear Warning** is a lightweight World of Warcraft addon for Guardian Druids. It provides a visual and audio alert when you are in combat but not in Bear Form and dynamically rebinds keys to shift you back.

#### Key Features:

*   **Custom Alert Icon**: A pulsing Bear Form icon pops up on your screen when you are out of form in combat.
*   **Emergency Keybinds**: Configure up to 3 custom hotkeys (e.g. `3` or `F`). Pressing any of them while the alert is active immediately casts Bear Form.
*   **Dynamic Binding Reversion**: When out of combat, or when already in Bear Form, the hotkeys are completely unbound from the addon and instantly revert to your normal action bar spells.
*   **Edge Screen Flash**: An optional full-screen vignette overlay that flashes when you are out of form.
*   **Audio Alerts**: Plays repeating sound warnings that stop automatically once you shift.
*   **Zone Restrictions**: Checkboxes to enable or disable the alert specifically in the open **World**, PvE **Instances** (delves, dungeons, raids), or **PvP** (battlegrounds and arenas).

#### Customization Options (Type `/bw` or `/bearwarning`):

*   Toggle visual alert, border glow, text, and screen flash independently.
*   Adjust icon size, opacity, and text size.
*   Customize the screen flash and border glow colors.
*   Change the warning sound file, repeating interval, and output channel.
*   Configure a fade-out duration for the alert so it automatically hides after a few seconds.
